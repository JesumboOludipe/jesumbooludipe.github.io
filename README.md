### Cross-Selling Opportunities in Banking — Python & Power BI

[View Project](https://github.com/JesumboOludipe/PortfolioProjects/tree/main/Cross%20Selling%20Recommendations%20for%20Banking%20Products) · [View Dashboard](https://app.powerbi.com/view?r=eyJrIjoiMzA2NDlkOTItZWQyYi00YmQwLWIyZTAtOTZjOTFjY2Y1OTdkIiwidCI6ImRmODY3OWNkLWE4MGUtNDVkOC05OWFjLWM4M2VkN2ZmOTVhMCJ9)

Analysed over 13 million customer records from a Spanish bank to uncover behavioural patterns and identify cross-selling opportunities. Performed end-to-end analysis including data cleaning, feature engineering, and exploratory analysis using Python (Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib), then visualised findings in Power BI.

**Key findings:** The majority of customers owned 0–2 products, presenting significant cross-selling potential. Current accounts and debit cards were most commonly held, while savings accounts and guarantees were least adopted — surfacing clear upsell targets. Delivered 10 prioritised cross-selling recommendations alongside a strategy covering targeted campaigns, product bundling, and customer experience improvements.

### Movie Gross Revenue Correlation — Python

[View Code](https://github.com/JesumboOludipe/PortfolioProjects/blob/main/Data%20Correlation%20in%20Python%20-%20Movies%20Dataset.ipynb)

Analysed 6,820 films released between 1986 and 2016 to identify the factors most correlated with box office gross revenue. Used Python (Pandas, NumPy, Seaborn, Matplotlib) for data wrangling, correlation analysis, and visualisation.

**Key findings:** Budget, production company, director, and user ratings showed the strongest correlations with gross revenue — insights applicable to production investment decisions and marketing strategy.
